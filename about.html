  ⚜ About Us ⚜  

Hands is a student community, The Hands gathered more than 200 students under the theme of helping each other maximize their effectiveness and efficiency . The Hands activities included Charity, Awareness, Entertainment, and Human Development Programs and Events . The Hands target is to help students and change their minds and lives and trying to get the students out the mood of college . The Hands is a community service club in the AAST that aims at providing Entertainment, Charity, Awareness, and Human Development programs. The Hands society has been found in 2002 and has attracted many students. Hands has organized many social and athletic events. They adopted a campaign for promoting poor villages in Alexandria. Also, Hands shares in Collecting Clothes campaign annually.


Our History:

The Hands is a non-profit student organization at the Arab Academy for Science and Technology & Maritime Transport. It was founded in fall 2009


Founded in :

In Arab Academy for Science and Technology & Maritime Transport.
Abou-Qir Campus


Hands Goals:

Hands organizes each semester HC (Hands Cup) in different sports (Football, Basketball, and Table-tennis) and celebrates the winners with HC trophy in a fruitful gathering.
Hands Vision is achieving deep relationships between students through a self-sufficient community that will benefit the students’ educational and social life.
Hands Goal is gathering the AAST student under the theme of helping each other.

Some of the Hands Activities include: Hands Cup, Ibrahim Elfiky’s programs at Alexandria, Cancer Campaign, and trips
